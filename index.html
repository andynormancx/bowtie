bowtie is a simple inspector for knockout bindings.

knockout is a great tool for model binding, but a lack of developer tool support for proper syntax highlighting can make picking out what's bound to what very difficult, especially in complex views.

bowtie is a simple bookmarklet that you drag onto your browser bookmark bar that will let you inspect bound elements with a single click and view their bindings. it will also let you watch the values of an element's bindings, whether they're simple observables or complex computed values.

to use bowtie, simply drag the below button onto your browser's bookmark bar. when you want to use it on a page, simply click the shortcut. hover any element with a knockout data binding and you'll see an hourglass. click it to view all the bindings that element has. if you'd like to watch the value of a binding, simply click 'watch.' that's all there is.

launch bowtie
